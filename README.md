# 🎤 OratorAI — AI Communication, Public Speaking & Accent Mastery Coach

OratorAI is an AI-powered speech training platform that helps users master **public speaking, communication clarity, and accent transformation**. It acts as a real-time speech coach that analyzes how you speak, gives structured feedback, and trains you to sound more confident, fluent, and globally understandable in any accent you choose.

From reducing filler words to mastering a **General American, New York, British RP, or custom accent**, OratorAI turns speech improvement into a measurable, gamified skill system.

---

# 🚀 Overview

Most language tools focus on grammar or vocabulary. OratorAI focuses on something deeper:

> **How you sound, how you structure ideas, and how effectively you communicate in real-time.**

It combines:
- Speech recognition
- NLP feedback systems
- Phonetic analysis
- Accent modeling
- AI coaching intelligence

to create a **personal communication transformation system**.

---

# 🎯 Key Features

## 🎙 1. Real-Time Speech Analysis Engine

OratorAI listens to your speech and evaluates:

- Filler words (“um”, “like”, “you know”)
- Speech clarity and articulation
- Speaking pace (words per minute)
- Pause patterns and hesitation
- Sentence structure quality
- Confidence indicators (tone + rhythm)

---

## 🤖 2. AI Communication Coach

After every speaking session, OratorAI generates structured feedback:

- Strengths summary
- Weaknesses breakdown
- Clarity score (0–100)
- Confidence score (0–100)
- Structure score (flow of ideas)
- Personalized improvement tips

Example:
> “Your ideas are strong, but your pacing is too fast and includes frequent filler words. Try adding structured pauses between key points.”

---

## 🌍 3. Accent Mastery Engine

Users can choose a target accent and train toward it:

Supported accents include:
- 🇺🇸 General American English
- 🗽 New York Accent
- 🇬🇧 British RP (Received Pronunciation)
- 🇦🇺 Australian English
- 🌏 Neutral Global English

---

### 🧠 How it works:

OratorAI breaks speech into:
- Phonemes (sound units)
- Intonation patterns
- Stress patterns
- Rhythm + pacing

Then compares it to a target accent model and generates:

- Accent similarity score (0–100)
- Phoneme-level corrections
- Intonation feedback
- Word pronunciation adjustments

---

## 🎧 4. Shadowing Practice Mode

A core training feature where users:

1. Listen to AI-generated sentence in target accent  
2. Repeat it in real time  
3. Receive accuracy score and correction feedback  

Example:
> “Your pronunciation of ‘water’ is too phonetic. In General American, it should sound closer to ‘wah-ter’.”

---

## 📊 5. Communication Progress Dashboard

Tracks long-term improvement:

- Filler word reduction over time
- Accent accuracy progression
- Speaking confidence trends
- Best recorded speeches
- Weakness heatmap (clarity, pacing, pronunciation)

---

## 🗣 6. Practice Modes

### 🎤 Impromptu Mode
Random speaking prompts for thinking-on-your-feet training.

### 💼 Interview Mode
Behavioral + technical interview simulations.

### 🎓 Presentation Mode
Structured speech building and delivery training.

### 🧠 Debate Mode
Argument construction, persuasion, and rebuttal scoring.

---

## 🔁 7. Accent Transformation Feedback Loop

Each session provides:

- Original speech playback
- Corrected pronunciation version
- Target accent transformation suggestion
- Side-by-side comparison analysis

# 📊 Scoring System

OratorAI evaluates speech using weighted metrics:

| Metric | Weight |
|--------|--------|
| Clarity | 25% |
| Structure | 20% |
| Confidence | 20% |
| Filler Word Usage | 15% |
| Pace Control | 10% |
| Accent Accuracy | 10% |

Final Score = 0–100 composite score
